Things you need to setup an aquarium
1. Fish tank 5-20lt
2. Water heater
3. Water filter
4. Cascade
5. Substrate
6. Thermometer
7. Aquarium rocks
8. Tetra aquasafe conditioner
9. Flourish
10. Aquarium bacteria
11. Fish
12. Plants
13. Led lamp
14. Tetra fish flakes
.
Steps to setup your aquarium
1. Rinse your tank
2. Add 1-inch of substrate
3. Fill up your tank with purified water, leaving and inch empty
4. Install your filter, thermometer (preferably on 24 Centigrade) turn in your cascade.
5. Add your rocks as decoration
6. Add water conditioner
7. Install your lamp
8. Add bacteria that day and the upcoming 3 weeks the same day
9. Wait 3-5 days to add any fish or plants
10. Plant the plants under the substrate leaving their roots underneath
11. Add your fish and feed them to reduce stress
12. Add approximately 1ml of flourish to help the plants grow, do it weekly
13. If any algae appear remove it immediately
14. Now you will have your beautiful, self-created ecosystem
