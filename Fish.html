2. Serpae Tetra (Hyphessobrycon eques) This type of fish is known for their unique colors similar to a flame. Serpae Tetra fish are easy to take care of, they usually get along with other types of species like the ones mentioned here, except for the betta. They originate in the Amazon basin, and they inhabit in different rivers like in Paraguay, the Guapore River and basins in Argentina and Brazil. This means they´re tropical fish and they need vegetation to leave peacefully, since they find it as a safety place. It is crucial that Serpae Tetra fish are kept in groups of 6 or more, because they can show aggression towards one of their own.
3. Albino cory catfish Albino cory catfish are an easy specie to take care of, it is recommended for beginners in aquariums. They are known to be peaceful with any other fish species in comparison to other catfish. The requirements to have one are very simple, all you need is a well oxygenated tank with vegetation and rocks to promote the production algae to have food resource. They will help you remove a great number of algae, which will keep your tank clean. By having all of these elements the fish can have a longer lifespan.
4. Betta Fish (CLICK ON THE IMAGE FOR AN EFFECT) This is one of the famous fish among the pet community for their astonishing colors. Betta fish are a highly intelligent specie, they can develop different personalities or behaviors. Males are very territorial specially with another male betta, in comparison to females who can live in groups called sororities. Male betta´s can adapt to live in a tank with other species like the Tetra Penguins and the Albino Cory Catfish, but it is crucial you have a tank of at least 5 gallons. Wild Betta fish inhabit canals in Thailand and Cambodia with densely vegetation, even though the fish we buy are already adapted to survive without vegetation, it is recommended to provide them with some, to reduce stress and increase lifespan
